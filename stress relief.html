Try these, it helps relieves some stress

1. Exercise:

Physical activity can be a great way to release stress and tension. Even a brisk walk or light jog can help to clear your mind and improve your mood.


2. Deep breathing:

Taking deep, slow breaths can help to reduce stress and anxiety by slowing your heart rate and lowering your blood pressure. Try inhaling deeply through your nose, holding the breath for a few seconds, and then exhaling slowly through your mouth.


3. Meditation:

Practicing meditation can help to quiet your mind and reduce stress. Find a quiet space, sit comfortably, and focus on your breath or a calming image or word.


4. Listening to music:

Listening to calming music can help to reduce stress and anxiety. Choose music that you find relaxing and soothing.


5. Spending time in nature:

Spending time in nature, such as taking a walk in the park or hiking in the mountains, can help to reduce stress and improve your mood. The sights and sounds of nature can have a calming effect on the mind and body.


Remember, it's important to find what works best for you. Try experimenting with different stress-relieving techniques until you find the ones that work best for you.
